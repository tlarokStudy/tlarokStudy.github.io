About Us

We created this platform with one goal: to make it easier for night-shift workers in Amsterdam to find good food - fast.

Whether you're a nurse finishing a 12 hour shift, a delivery driver between routes, or a bartender wrapping up late, you shouldn't have to guess which restaurants are open. That's why we provide up-to-date listings of late-night food options, with real-time info on hours, menus, prices, and dietary choices.No more wasted trips. No more cold vending machine meals. Just reliable, local food when you need it most.
